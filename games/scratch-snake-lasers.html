Snake & Lasers

About the Game

Created by @-TotallyEpic-, Snake & Lasers is an exciting twist on the classic snake game. This version adds a new dimension of challenge with laser elements, making it more engaging and thrilling than the traditional snake game. Made within 24 hours, it has quickly become a community favorite, earning Top Loved status and inspiring numerous remixes.

How to Play

  • Control: Use the arrow keys to navigate your snake through the game area.
  • Objective: Collect white pellets to increase your score and length.
  • Dangers: Avoid hitting yourself, lasers, bullets, and the walls.
  • Important: Be careful not to turn too quickly, as you will die!
  • Goal: Try to get a stupendously amazing score!
