Snake

About the Game

Snake is an innovative educational version of the classic snake game, created using the Scratch programming platform. This version offers a unique perspective on the traditional snake game, combining educational value with engaging gameplay. It's perfect for those who want to learn programming concepts while enjoying a classic arcade experience.

How to Play

  • Controls: Use the arrow keys to guide your snake through the game area.
  • Objective: Collect food items to grow your snake while avoiding collisions with walls and your own tail.
  • Features: Experience a unique take on the classic snake game, designed to help understand programming concepts.

Tips for Success

  • Take your time to understand the game mechanics and controls.
  • Plan your movements carefully to avoid getting trapped.
  • Pay attention to your snake's length and position to prevent self-collision.
