Zig Snake

About the Game

Zig Snake is a fast-paced arcade game where your snake zigzags forward nonstop! It's the ultimate test of your reaction speed, focus, and timing. Dive into this retro-style snake game, challenge yourself, and try to beat your high score!

How to Play

  • Controls: The snake moves forward automatically. Tap or click to make the snake instantly change direction (left or right).
  • Objective: Avoid crashing into the walls or falling off the track. Score points by staying alive as long as possible.
  • Features: Simple one-tap controls, endless zigzag track, fast-paced gameplay, and high score challenge.

Tips for Success

  • Stay focused and anticipate turns ahead of time.
  • Tap with good timing to avoid sharp corners and sudden obstacles.
