Snake Classic

About the Game

Snake Classic: A modern twist on the nostalgic snake game. Navigate your snake through six unique modes: Classic, Box, Tunnel, Mill, Condo, and Rail. Collect fruits and gems, customize your snake with skins, and enjoy multi-language support. Compete on personal leaderboards and play on both PC and mobile. Fun and engaging for all ages!

How to Play

  • Objective: Control your snake to collect fruit and gems while avoiding collisions with walls and your own tail.
  • PC Controls: Movement: Use the WASD keys or Arrow keys to control the direction of the snake.
  • Mouse Control: Swipe with your mouse in the direction you want the snake to move.
  • Mobile Controls: Swipe up, down, left, or right on the screen to control the direction of the snake.
  • Features: Six unique game modes, customizable snake skins, fruits and gems, multi-language support, personal leaderboards, and cross-platform play.

Tips for Success

  • Plan your moves ahead to avoid getting trapped by your own tail.
  • Try all six modes to master different challenges and layouts.
  • Collect as many fruits and gems as possible to boost your score and unlock new skins.
  • Practice both keyboard and swipe controls for the best experience on any device.
