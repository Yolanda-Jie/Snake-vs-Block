Helix Snake

About the Game

Helix Snake is an exciting arcade-style game that puts a unique twist on the classic snake concept. Navigate through a mesmerizing spiral track while collecting points and avoiding obstacles. The game combines simple controls with challenging gameplay, making it both accessible and engaging for players of all skill levels.

How to Play

  • Controls: Use your mouse or touch controls to guide the snake through the spiral track.
  • Objective: Collect points while navigating through the increasingly challenging spiral path.
  • Features: Experience dynamic speed changes and various obstacles that test your reflexes and timing.

Tips for Success

  • Start slow and get comfortable with the controls before attempting faster speeds.
  • Pay attention to the track's curvature and plan your movements accordingly.
  • Don't get greedy with points - sometimes it's better to take a safer route.
