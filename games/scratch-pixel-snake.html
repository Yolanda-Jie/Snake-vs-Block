Scratch Pixel Snake

About the Game

Scratch Pixel Snake is a retro-style version of the classic snake game, developed using the Scratch programming platform. This version features charming pixel art graphics that bring a nostalgic feel to the traditional snake game. It combines educational programming concepts with engaging gameplay, making it perfect for those who appreciate retro gaming aesthetics while learning about game development.

How to Play

  • Controls: Use the arrow keys to navigate your pixel snake through the game area.
  • Objective: Collect pixel food items to grow your snake while avoiding collisions with walls and your own tail.
  • Features: Experience a retro-style take on the classic snake game with pixel art graphics.

Tips for Success

  • Take your time to understand the game mechanics and controls.
  • Plan your movements carefully to avoid getting trapped.
  • Pay attention to your snake's length and position to prevent self-collision.
  • Enjoy the retro pixel art style while playing!
