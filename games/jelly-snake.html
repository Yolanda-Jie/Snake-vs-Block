Jelly Snake

About the Game

Jelly Snake is a charming and unique take on the classic snake game, featuring a wobbly, bouncy jelly snake as the main character. Navigate through colorful levels, collect delicious treats, and watch your jelly snake grow in this fun and engaging arcade experience. The game combines classic snake mechanics with delightful jelly physics for a fresh and entertaining gameplay experience.

How to Play

  • Controls: Use arrow keys or WASD to guide your wobbly jelly snake through the levels.
  • Objective: Collect treats to grow your jelly snake while avoiding obstacles and maintaining control of your bouncy movements.
  • Features: Experience unique jelly physics that make your snake bounce and wobble in a delightful way.

Tips for Success

  • Get used to the jelly physics and how your snake bounces and wobbles.
  • Plan your movements ahead to account for the bouncy nature of your snake.
  • Take advantage of the jelly physics to reach treats in hard-to-reach places.
