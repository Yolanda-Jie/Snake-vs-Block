Snake

About the Game

Created by Dibbo, this version of the classic snake game offers a smooth and challenging gameplay experience. The game runs even smoother when downloaded. It's a perfect blend of classic snake mechanics with modern gameplay elements, making it an engaging experience for players of all skill levels.

How to Play

  • Controls: Use the arrow keys to navigate your snake through the game area.
  • Objective: Collect food items to grow your snake while avoiding collisions with walls and your own tail.
  • Features: Experience smooth gameplay and challenging mechanics in this well-crafted version.

Tips for Success

  • Take your time to get used to the smooth controls.
  • Plan your route ahead to avoid getting trapped by walls or your own tail.
  • Try to beat your high score with each playthrough.
