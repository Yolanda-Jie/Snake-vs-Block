About the Game

Snake vs Block is an engaging and fast-paced arcade game that tests your reflexes and strategic planning. You control a snake composed of individual balls. Your primary objective is to collect more balls to extend the length of your snake while navigating through a path littered with numbered blocks.

How to Play

  • Control Your Snake: Use your mouse (on desktop) or finger (on mobile) to swipe left or right, guiding the snake's movement.
  • Collect Balls: Glide through the glowing orbs marked with a plus (+) sign. Each orb collected adds to your snake's length, making it stronger.
  • Break the Blocks: Strategically hit the numbered blocks in your path. The number on a block indicates how many segments of your snake will be consumed to break it. Aim for blocks with lower numbers.
  • Survive: If your snake's length drops to zero after hitting a block, or if you attempt to break a block that requires more segments than you possess, the game ends.
  • Score High: Your score is determined by how many blocks you break and how far you travel. Challenge yourself to beat your personal best!

Tips for Success

  • Always prioritize collecting balls, especially when your snake is short.
  • Plan your path ahead to target blocks with the lowest numbers.
  • Sometimes it's wiser to avoid a series of high-numbered blocks than to risk losing too much length.
  • Quick, precise swipes are key to navigating tight spots.
